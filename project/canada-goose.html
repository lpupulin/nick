[insights]
Through animation, we captured a shift in identity for Canada Goose, one rooted in material innovation and craft. The film focused on the product, but carried a deeper narrative: a move away from animal-based materials toward a more considered future. Every frame was designed to reflect precision, texture, and intention, balancing the heritage of the brand with the progressive values behind the new range. The result was a visual statement that marked change without losing continuity.
