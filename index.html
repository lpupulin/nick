[BIO]
A multi-disciplined creative, grounded in the fundamentals of design and driven by a passion for digital image-making, brand strategy, sound design, and the undercurrents of creative culture.
His practice lives at the intersection of digital art, purpose-led storytelling, and visual innovation spanning moving image to raw craft.
With an interdisciplinary curiosity, he has built a diverse career across agency communication, moving image design, retail activations, and brand design for Adidas Global. His most persistent pursuit has been on supporting reshape and push sports and fashion storytelling by using CGI as a core medium, supporting brands elevate their visual language.
